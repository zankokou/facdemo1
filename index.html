Welcome to Futures Accounting & Compliance Inc.

GIVE OUR TEAM OF FORMER NFA AUDITORS AND CPAs A CHANCE TO DEMONSTRATE EXCELLENCE IN THE AREA OF ACCOUNTING & COMPLIANCE.

Futures Accounting & Compliance (FAC) was formed in 1992 to provide custom accounting and compliance services by highly qualified professionals with extensive regulatory backgrounds and the sincere desire to contribute to their client's success. It is a dynamic accounting and compliance firm serving the Commodities and Securities industry for over 20 years. It's philosophy centers on providing absolute quality to every client regardless of fees generated.


As compliance experts, we will take the time to educate and assist with all regulatory matters so that you can concentrate on growing your business. Our vast accounting and compliance experience helps us provide a wide range of services from compiling financial statements for funds and IBs and track records for CTAs to detail compliance reviews and certified audits.


Our emphasis will always be to add value beyond that implied by simply accounting for monthly transaction. We currently serve as accountants for over 100 Pool Operators (Commodity and Hedge Funds), Commodity Trading Advisors, Introducing Brokers and Futures Commission Merchants all over the country and overseas. We are a boutique firm that provides expert compliance and accounting services and our size allows us to provide personalized service to each of our clients. We look forward to a chance to discuss your accounting and compliance needs.
